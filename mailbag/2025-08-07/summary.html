MLB Mailbag: Schwarber, Braves, Story, Naylor, Tucker, Rockies

MLB Trade Rumors Daily Digest

Key Insights

  • Kyle Schwarber is set to hit free agency this offseason after a career-best season in Philadelphia. The Phillies may look to extend him on a 4-year, $125M contract ($31.25M AAV) to keep him from reaching the open market.
  • Schwarber has already reached 4 WAR this season, a new career-high, and is on pace for over 5 WAR. His previous best was 3.3 WAR in 2021.
  • Only two DHs age 33+ have gotten 4-year deals in free agency (Nelson Cruz, Victor Martinez), so a 4-year, $125M contract could be enough to retain Schwarber.
  • The Phillies have room in their $185M CBT payroll for 2023 to give Schwarber a $30M AAV contract, though there are questions about whether that would be a good long-term investment.
